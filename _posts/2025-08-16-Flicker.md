**Overview:**
Submission for the 7 day 2025 Epic Megajam. Entered for the 'One Man Army' and 'Use It or Lose It' special modifiers.

Some audio assets come from weloveindies, all other game assets made during jam.

Developed for the theme "However vast the darkness, we must supply our own light"

Only a single 512x512 texture is sampled during the game.

Animation for character baked from new procedural locomotor plugin introduced in UE 5.6 (excluding additive layers).

**Platform and Engine:**  
   Unreal Engine 5 for PCVR platforms

See the [itch.io page](https://itch.io/jam/2025-epic-megajam/rate/3985332 "flicker itch page") to play.
